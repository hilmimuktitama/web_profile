---
title: "Program Truth Over Green Status"
description: "Why the work underneath a status update matters more than the color on the parent ticket."
pubDate: 2026-05-13
tags:
  - program-truth
  - delivery
---

Program reporting gets risky when the visible status is cleaner than the work underneath it.

The useful question is not whether a parent ticket is green. It is whether the task-level evidence supports that call, and whether every blocker has an owner, a date, and a next decision.

Good reporting makes uncertainty visible while there is still time to act.
