This case note keeps the private details out and focuses on the shape of the work.

## What Changed

- Delivery signal was scattered across plans, team updates, and execution tools.
- Stakeholders needed clearer status without adding more meetings.
- Blockers, owners, dates, and next actions were pulled into a simpler review rhythm.
- Updates became easier to trust because facts and assumptions were no longer mixed together.
